# app to feed pets 

## Overview
A mobile app that automates pet feeding schedules and sends reminders to ensure pets are fed on time consistently. The app tracks feeding history and allows customization of feeding schedules for multiple pets.

## Problem
Pet owners often forget feeding times, overfeed, or struggle to coordinate feeding schedules, leading to inconsistent nutrition and potential health issues

## Target User
Pet owners who want to maintain consistent feeding schedules and may have multiple pets or busy lifestyles

## Features
- Custom feeding schedules per pet
- Push notifications for feeding times
- Feeding history tracking
- Multiple pet profiles
- Portion size recommendations
